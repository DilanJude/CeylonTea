Health Benefits

If you are buying tea after considering the health benefits. You would consider buying a shade grown tea. Spending time in the shade increases the amount of Chlorophyll and with it, the nutrients in the leaves. The more the chlorophyll presence in the tea , it makes it more rich in taste also. Black tea also comes under the shade grown tea while it gives a fruity taste. Though shade grown tea is health benefecial it's cost is abit high.There are many types of Shade grown teas and they are as such :

  1. Matcha Tea
  2. Keemun Tea
  3. Gyokuro

Quality

In terms of Quality Japanese tea, white tea are taking the upper ranks due to the special way they are made and for the nutrients they contain. You should consider these teas if you are considering quality:

  1. White Hair Silver Tea
  2. Golden Monkey Tea
  3. Gyokuro

Caffeine Content

There are many rumours & misinformations out there about the content of caffeine in tea and much of it comes from many reliable sources. Basically tea types don't determine the content of caffeine they have in them. The caffeine content depends on number of factors such as :

  1. Roasting ( They are generally lower in caffeine but not always ).
  2. Tea Leave Maturity( the more larger the tea are mature , then they have less caffeine ).
  3. Sleeping time of leaves.

Stress Relief & Relaxation

Teas are known for many benefits , one being relaxation. Jasmine teas are best known for relaxation because it's flower has shown to have an additional calming effect. Generally herbal teas are good for relaxation & aiding you in sleep.We reccommend you the following teas for relaxation and stress relief :

  1. Jasmine Green Tea
  2. Jasmine Silver Needle Tea
